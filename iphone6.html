• The Iphone 6 & 6 plus

    • The Iphone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus are smartphones that were designed and marketed by Apple.They are the eighth generation of the iPhone, were announced on September 9, 2014, and released on September 19, 2014. The iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus were themselves replaced as the devices of the iPhone series by the iPhone 6S and iPhone 6S Plus on September 9, 2015. The iPhone 6 and 6 Plus include larger 4.7 and 5.5 inches (120 and 140 mm) displays, a faster processor, upgraded cameras, improved LTE and Wi-Fi connectivity. The iPhone 6 and 6 plus were discontinued on September 7 2016 when the 7 and 7 plus came out. The design of the iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus were based on the design of the iPad Air with a glass front that is curved around the edges of the display, and an aluminum back. The back is built of 6000-series aluminum. Both models come in gold, silver, and "space gray" finishes. The iPhone 6 has a thickness of 6.9 millimetres, while the iPhone 6 Plus is 7.1 mm in thickness; both are thinner than the iPhone 5c and iPhone 5s, with the iPhone 6 being Apple's thinnest phone to date.
